The Arts:

Before I could walk, I'd pick up some crayons and doodle. Art has been an integral part of my life and has completely changed the lense of my perspective on the world. I love working with different mediums like acrylic, pencil, charcol, oil pastels, digital, and 3D modelling. I believe art fosters internal and emotional connections between humans through visual appeal. During the COVID-19 quarantine, I started a Instagram art account to track my progress and now I'm currently working on launching my online art business as well as my personal art portfolio. Feel free to check out my Instagram account!

@lyiwae :)



Taekwondo:

Taekwondo first became an integral part of my life nearly 8 years ago. I am a 3rd Dan Black Belt, certified by the World Taekwondo Federation. This sport has strengthened both my physical and mental health. Pre-COVID, I volunteered as an Assistant Instructor during both regular classes and the summer camps. More recently, I've begun giving back to the Taekwondo community by teaching students as an Instructor. I teach students from ages 4-50+ and I absolutely love how rewarding it is.
